"There is" and "There are"


Use "there is" when you want to say that something exists or is in a place, and the thing you are talking about is singular or uncountable

For instance: "There is a bed in my room"

Use "there are" when you want to say that something exists or is in a place, and the thing you are talking about is plural.

For instance: "There are some cats in my yard
